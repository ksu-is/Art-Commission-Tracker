# Art-Commission-Tracker
This is an art commission tracker that can help freelance artists with the organization and management of their art commissions while also providing statistical insights into their projects and earnings.

## Features
- Add, edit, and delete commission entries
- Filter commissions by status and  sort by deadline
- Receive a summary report of total commissions and complete income
- Commission statistics
- Calculating commission pricing
